R Project #2
Perceived usefulness of work by employees: The European case

This article showcases how the open-source software R processes, analyzes, and extracts relevant information from large datasets. Using Eurostat data, we will streamline the information to create a two-variable database focusing on job usefulness perceptions among European employees aged 25-64 in 2015. Our process involves describing the initial database, identifying significant information using inference tools, and constructing a refined database. The resulting "useful work rate" reflects employees' perceptions of their job's usefulness, synthesized into a meaningful percentage.
